about me

Introduction

I'm a new Thinkful student looking to brush up on my skills. I used to work at a non-profit exchange organization. I eventually changed gears and went back to school to study multimedia design and web development. For the last 7 odd years I've worked at digital and marketing agencies on various web and email projects.

I'm a thoughtful and resourceful person with a mind for creative problem-solving. And I love to understand how things work. That's why I chose to pursue this career.

Continuing to learn is important to me. I can't be happy without a little challenge. At any given point, I have at least a couple projects brewing.

Where are you from?

I was born and reared in the Willamette Valley, Oregon. I live in Portland now. I've spent some time in other places in the Pacific Northwest. Vancouver (BC), Olympia and San Juan Island are all dear to my heart.
